Life in the Intertidal Zone

What is the intertidal zone?
It's an area which is under water during high tide and above water during low tide.
What might I see in Maine's intertidal zone?
You likely will see barnacles, periwinkles, and seashore springtails.
Photo Name and Details
Barnacle
Suspension feeding barnacles are marine arthropods related to lobsters and crabs.
Periwinkle
This marine gastropod is one of the over 200 types of sea snails.
Seashore Springtail
The bodies of these tiny (3mm) arthropods are covered with hydrophobic hairs.
